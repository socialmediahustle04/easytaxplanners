# EasyTaxPlanners Product Brief

## Simple Summary

EasyTaxPlanners should be a modern CA-led compliance website for people who need practical tax and business filing help.

This is not only an income tax filing product. The client conversation shows the business should cover:
- Income Tax Filing
- GST Return Filing
- Company Return Filing
- Registrations
- Financial Statements / Balance Sheet Preparation

The strongest market angle is:

> Help new businesses and individuals handle tax compliance without confusion, delay, or hidden surprises.

## What We Learned From The Client Conversation

The client wants the website to be service-led, simple, and not overloaded with too many options.

Initial services should be:

1. Income Tax Filing
2. GST Return Filing
3. Company Return Filing
4. Registration
   - GST Registration
   - Company Incorporation
5. Financial Statements / Balance Sheet Preparation

Additional registrations such as Udyam, FSSAI, trademark, copyright, and patent can be added later after the team is ready to deliver them.

## Real Target Customer

The client clearly said big audit clients usually already have an existing CA.

The better target customer is:
- New business owners
- Startup founders
- IT consultants and online service providers
- Small agencies
- Newly incorporated companies
- GST-registered businesses
- People needing balance sheets for loans
- People with pending returns or messy compliance
- People whose previous filing was done incorrectly

## Emotional Insight

For salaried taxpayers, the emotion is:

> Avoid tax notice anxiety.

For small business owners, the stronger emotion is:

> Run your business peacefully without compliance tension.

This website should sell assurance, clarity, and reliable CA-led support.

## Trust Strategy

Trust is the biggest challenge.

The website must answer:
- Who is behind this?
- What services are actually handled?
- What documents are required?
- What happens after I submit the form?
- Will I know the scope before I pay?
- Can I speak to someone on WhatsApp?

If ICAI/CA advertising rules allow it, the About page can include the CA's name, photo, qualification, and experience.

If not, use safer wording:
- "CA-led compliance support"
- "Handled by qualified tax professionals"
- "Professional guidance for income tax, GST, company filing, and registrations"

The CA owner should confirm the final About-page wording with a senior CA or ICAI guidance before launch.

## Differentiation

Large platforms sell speed and automation.

EasyTaxPlanners should sell:
- CA-led clarity
- Simple service categories
- Clear document checklist
- Practical support for new businesses
- Proper filing and compliance guidance
- Human follow-up via WhatsApp/contact

## MVP Success Criteria

The website succeeds if:
- A non-technical business owner understands what services are offered within 10 seconds.
- The homepage creates trust without feeling like a generic CA template.
- A visitor can quickly submit a service enquiry.
- The website is ready for SEO pages and daily content.
- No competitor assets or copied designs are used.
