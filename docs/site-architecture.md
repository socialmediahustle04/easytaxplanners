## Homepage Section Detail

### 1. Hero

Goal:
Make the visitor immediately understand that this is a tax/compliance services website.

Content:
- Headline
- Subheadline
- Primary/secondary CTA
- Hero dashboard visual
- Trust strip

Trust strip:
- CA-led guidance
- Clear document checklist
- WhatsApp support
- Review before filing

### 2. Core Services

Six service cards:
- Income Tax Filing
- GST Return Filing
- Company Return Filing
- GST Registration
- Company Incorporation
- Financial Statements

### 3. Who We Help

Segments:
- New business owners
- IT consultants and freelancers
- GST registered businesses
- Private limited companies
- People with pending filings
- People needing balance sheet for loans

### 4. How It Works

Steps:
1. Tell us what service you need
2. Get a document checklist
3. Review scope and timeline
4. Filing/preparation work begins
5. Final confirmation and support

### 5. Trust Section

Explain:
- CA-led professional guidance
- No sensitive details required in first form
- Clear scope before work starts
- Practical support through WhatsApp

### 6. Problem-Led CTA

Questions:
- GST return pending?
- Company filing delayed?
- Need balance sheet for bank loan?
- Unsure which return applies?
- Starting a company or GST registration?

CTA:
- "Speak to us"

### 7. Resources Preview

Show latest educational articles.

Initial resource categories:
- Income Tax
- GST
- Company Compliance
- Registrations
- Business Finance

### 8. Final CTA

Headline:
> Need help with tax or business compliance?

CTA:
> Send Enquiry

## Lead Form

Fields:
- Name
- WhatsApp number
- Email optional
- Service needed
- Message

Service dropdown:
- Income Tax Filing
- GST Return Filing
- Company Return Filing
- GST Registration
- Company Incorporation
- Financial Statements
- Other

Do not ask for:
- PAN
- Aadhaar
- GST portal password
- Income tax portal password
- Bank statements
- Uploads

These can be collected later after trust and scope are established.
